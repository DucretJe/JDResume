Senior Site Reliability Engineer - Cloud Infrastructure

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Site Reliability Engineer to join our growing platform team. You will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining our cloud infrastructure at scale.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable Kubernetes-based infrastructure
- Develop and maintain Infrastructure as Code using Terraform and Ansible
- Implement GitOps workflows using ArgoCD or Flux
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines with GitHub Actions
- Ensure high availability and reliability of production systems
- Implement comprehensive monitoring and observability solutions using Grafana, Prometheus
- Collaborate with development teams to improve application reliability
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response
- Mentor junior team members

Required Skills:
- 3+ years of experience in SRE or DevOps role
- Strong experience with Kubernetes in production environments
- Expertise in Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible)
- Proficiency in at least one programming language (Python, Go, or similar)
- Experience with GitOps practices and tools (ArgoCD, Flux)
- Strong knowledge of Linux systems administration
- Experience with cloud providers (GCP, AWS, or Azure)
- Experience with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, Loki)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with multi-cloud environments
- Knowledge of security best practices
- Experience with service mesh technologies
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Certifications (CKA, CKAD, Terraform, or cloud certifications)

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and benefits
- Remote-friendly work environment
- Learning and development budget
- Modern tech stack and challenging projects
- Collaborative and supportive team culture
